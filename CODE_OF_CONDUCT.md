# Code of Conduct

Sanchr contributors are expected to keep project spaces professional,
respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good faith while still asking for evidence and clear reasoning.
- Respect privacy and do not share private security reports, credentials, user
  data, or internal operational details.
- Keep disagreement technical and specific.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately disruptive behavior in issues, pull requests, discussions, or
  security reports.
- Using project spaces to disclose vulnerabilities before maintainers have had
  a reasonable chance to respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close discussions, block accounts, or restrict
participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

Report conduct concerns privately to `security@sanchr.com`.
